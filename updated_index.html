About Liquital Capital

Liquital Capital (Pty) Ltd (“Liquital”) provides funding solutions to South African Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) with valid contracts to supply goods and services to both private and public sector entities. These include government departments, state-owned companies, and municipalities.

Our Mission

Liquital seeks to solve SMEs' access to funding by offering innovative solutions that unlock capacity to fulfil contractual obligations. Through our collaborative approach, we assist SMEs in business growth, working capital, job creation, asset acquisition, competitive advantage, and access to expertise.

Our Services

Range of Liquital service offerings includes Purchase Order Financing, Invoice Discounting, and Contract Financing.
