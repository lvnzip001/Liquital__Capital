What you need to apply for business funding

1 Turnover

To apply for business funding, your business needs to have a turnover greater than R1 million (R100 000 per month) over the past 12 months.


2 Business History

Your last 12 months of business bank statements. This helps our algorithm and proprietary tools to give you a better response on your application.


3 Ownership

To be eligible for funding, your business must be a registered entity that has been fully operational for at least 12 months and must not be structured as a sole proprietorship, trust, or partnership. Additionally, the current business ownership or management team should have been established and in place for more than 6 months.
