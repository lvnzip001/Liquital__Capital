Welcome to Liquital Funding Application

We are here to support your business growth and success. Before you begin the application process, here's what you can expect:

  • Step 1: Company Information - You will provide essential details about your company, including its name, registration number, address, BEE rating, sector.
  • Step 2: Managing Director Details - Enter information about the managing director, including name, email address, phone number, and ID number.
  • Step 3: Off-taker Details - Provide information about off-takers, including company name, sector, contact person, designation, email address, phone number.
  • Step 4: Contract/Purchase Order Information - Details about the contract or purchase order, including issue date, ending date, contract number, description, value, and payment terms.
  • Step 5: Invoice Information (if applicable) - If you have invoices, provide information like issue date, settlement date, invoice amount, and job description.
  • Step 6: Funding Request - Specify the funding amount required, the intended use of funds, and the expected repayment date.
  • Step 7: Track Record - Share your track record by providing client information, job descriptions, amounts, and contact details.
  • Step 8: Invoices Outstanding - If you have outstanding invoices, provide client details, amounts, contact details, and settlement dates.
  • Step 9: Live Contracts - List live contracts with client information, job descriptions, contract values, and contact details.
  • Step 10: Company Asset Register - Enter information about company assets, including date of acquisition, asset description, purchase amount, and book value.
  • Step 11: Company Liability Register - Record details of company liabilities, including lender name, amount financed, outstanding amount, and settlement date.
  • Step 12: Director(s) Asset & Liability Statement - Provide information about director(s) assets and liabilities.
  • Step 13: Documents to be provided - Check the compulsory documents to be provided as part of your application.
